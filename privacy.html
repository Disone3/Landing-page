Privacy Policy

3Nil Sports, LLC (“we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide when you opt in to receive text messages from us, including marketing messages, notifications, reminders, and other similar communications.

By providing your phone number and opting in, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign up to receive text messages, we may collect:

  • Your mobile phone number
  • Your name (if you choose to provide it)
  • Other information you voluntarily provide (such as email address, preferences, or customer account details)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Send you text messages you have opted in to receive (such as promotions, offers, reminders, and alerts)
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Improve our services and tailor communications to your preferences
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

3. Consent and Opt-Out

  • By opting in, you agree to receive recurring text messages from 3Nil Sports, LLC at the mobile number you provide.
  • Message and data rates may apply.
  • You can opt out at any time by replying “STOP” to any message. After opting out, you may receive a one-time confirmation message, after which you will no longer receive texts from us.
  • To request help, reply “HELP” or contact us directly using the information below.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist us in delivering our text messaging services (such as messaging platforms or carriers)
  • If required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our customers and partners
  • No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal information against unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can remove it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

8. Contact Us

You may contact us at support@3nilsports.com.
